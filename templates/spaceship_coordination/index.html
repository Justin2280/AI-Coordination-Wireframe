About the Experiment

In this experiment, you will work as part of a 3-person crew to mine valuable minerals from asteroids across different star systems.

Your Role

  • Captain: Coordinates the team and makes strategic decisions
  • Navigator: Controls travel between systems and asteroids
  • Driller: Mines asteroids for minerals

Objective

Maximize your team's mineral points while managing your limited Power Units (PU) budget.

Getting Started
